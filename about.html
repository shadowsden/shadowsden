About Me

WHO I AM

Hi, I'm ShadowFolf — a writer, photographer, and creative based online. Shadow's Den is my little corner of the internet where I share my work and passions.

WHAT I DO

I write stories about wolf-fox hybrids and other creatures that live in the shadows. I also love photography, capturing the quiet and moody moments that most people walk past without noticing.

MY STORY

Shadow's Den started as a place to keep my writing and photography in one spot. Over time it grew into something I'm proud of — a space that feels genuinely mine.

GET IN TOUCH

The best way to reach me is through Discord. I love hearing from people who enjoy my work or just want to chat!
